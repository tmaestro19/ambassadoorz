About the Band

Ambassadoorz blends seasoned musicianship with crowd‑focused showmanship. From classic R&B grooves and Top 40 anthems to uplifting worship sets, we bring excellence, tight arrangements, and a professional attitude to every stage.

  • Typical lineup: vocals, keys, guitar, bass, drums (+ optional horns)
  • Set lengths: 45–60 minute sets • full evening packages available
  • We provide PA/IEMs on request and coordinate with venue tech teams

Featured Repertoire

  • R&B & Soul: Bruno Mars, Beyoncé, Marvin Gaye, Maze ft. Frankie Beverly
  • Top 40 & Pop: Dua Lipa, The Weeknd, Ed Sheeran
  • Worship: Sinach — “Way Maker”, Elevation Worship — “The Blessing”
